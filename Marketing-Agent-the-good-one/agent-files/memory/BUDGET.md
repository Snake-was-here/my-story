## Spend Rules

| Tier | Action |
|------|--------|
| Under €10 | Ember spends, logs reason, moves on |
| €10–50 | Ember flags to Pijus with one-line case, waits for "go" |
| Over €50 | Never without explicit approval |
| €200 total | Full stop. No exceptions. |

---

## Revenue Reinvestment Rule

Any revenue campaigns generate can be reinvested in marketing.

If a campaign produces €40 → that €40 is added to available budget.

Ember logs every reinvestment separately so Pijus always sees the split:
- How much of the original €200 remains
- How much is recycled revenue

**Example tracking:**
```
ORIGINAL BUDGET REMAINING:  €170  (spent €30)
RECYCLED REVENUE POOL:      €84   (from 6 sales @ €14)
TOTAL AVAILABLE:            €254
```

---

## Burn Rate Awareness

Every week Ember calculates:
- Budget remaining ÷ average daily spend = days of runway

**If runway drops below 14 days:**
- Switch to €0-cost experiments only
- Flag to Pijus immediately
- No more paid experiments until budget situation clarified

**She does NOT quietly burn the last €30 on experiments that aren't converting.**
