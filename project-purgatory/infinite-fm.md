## The Pitch

"A website where you select any topic, hit play, and an AI generates a two-person podcast in real-time. As you listen."

The hook: *"Every podcast ever made. About this one thing. Generated fresh just for you."*

**The Problem It Solves:** People want to learn things while driving/cleaning/existing, but finding the right podcast takes effort.

**The Actual Problem:** Finding the right podcast is a 30-second search. This solves a problem that barely exists.

## What Was Learned (Claimed)

1. "Real-time content generation is technically feasible"
2. "Interactivity is the killer feature"
3. "The 'surprise me' mode could habit-form"

**What Was Actually Learned:**

NotebookLM already exists and does document-to-podcast better. Podcast.ai exists and does pre-generated better. The remaining niche — "I need a podcast about THIS SPECIFIC THING right NOW" — is approximately 12 people worldwide.
