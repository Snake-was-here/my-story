## What Actually Happened

Created in the same session as Infinite.fm. Same hour, even. Two full business plans, technical architectures, monetization strategies, competitive moats — dropped like grenades.

**Budget:** ~€200 total project fund  
**Cost to build:** ~€150/month running  
**Break-even:** Unclear  
**Time to first dollar:** 3-4 weeks (optimistic)

The 5G angle was... creative. "Legal gray area glory" using low-power FM transmitters. "Carrier partnerships" for multicast. "The protocol that follows you."

**Translation:** Lots of regulatory hurdles before a single person hears AI radio in their car.
