## What Survived: The Pattern 🔄

**Research > Action.** 

It's his superpower and his kryptonite. He can research a life change into the ground. He can analyze the perfect escape until the escape window closes. He can write 4,000 words about "The Frequency Shift" without ever shifting frequencies.

**Planning > Booking.**

He planned two entire relocations — budgets, visa requirements, accommodation options, meditation schedules, daily burn rates, comparison matrices — and booked exactly zero flights.

**The Pattern Is The Point:**

He doesn't want to escape. He wants to *want* to escape.

The planning is the drug. The research is the hit. The document is the proof that he's "working on it."

Actually going? That's scary. Actually going means:
- No more research phase to hide in
- No more "almost ready" to lean on
- Just... being there. In India. In Vietnam. Outside the comfort zone.

Much safer to have beautiful plans that die than messy realities that disappoint.
