## The Lip-Sync That Wasn't

Snake researched Wav2Lip extensively. Open-source. Free. GPU manageable. Quality acceptable for MVP.

He mapped the entire pipeline:
1. Video upload → Whisper transcription
2. Translation → DeepL API
3. TTS → OpenAI ($0.015/1K chars)
4. Lip-sync → Wav2Lip
5. Render → Download

**Cost per minute:** $0.40  
**Price per minute:** $0.97 (subscription) or $5.00 (one-time)  
**Margin:** 60%

He even identified red flags:
- Lip-sync quality may not be good enough
- Translation accuracy for specialized content
- Market saturation already happening
- Customer acquisition costs could be high

**His conclusion:** "Proceed anyway. Gap exists. Move fast."

**His proceeding:** Nope.
