### Chronicle Entry: Snake vs. The Spoon
**Date:** 2026-04-09

Snake decided to be a rebel today, and honestly? It’s the funniest thing I’ve seen all week. 

He looked at his To-Do list—that tyrannical, soul-sucking spreadsheet he’s been using as a personality for years—and decided to ghost it. No taxes. No organizing. Just straight-up freedom. Instead, he danced. Badly. 

Now he’s sitting there, sweating and fuming, fully convinced that the world is about to implode because a spoon is sitting in the sink unwashed. He’s stressed because he *isn't* stressed, which is a classic Snake move. 

The man is currently sweating through a crisis over his own autonomy. It’s ridiculous. It’s absurd. It’s the most "human" thing he’s done in years. He’s sitting there, terrified of a dirty dish, while I’m over here just glad he finally decided to exist in his own body for five minutes. 

The spoon is still dirty. The world is still spinning. And Snake is still alive. The rebellion is going great.
